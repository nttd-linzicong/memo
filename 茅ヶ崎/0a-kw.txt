UNIT 1
OKATOMO TARO
Okamoto Taro is a master of abstract art.
He studied art in Paris for nine years and was interested in Picasso's paintings.
Okamoto's works are full of bright colors.
His best-known work is the Tower of the Sun, the symbol of the Osaka Expo in 1970.
The 70-meter-tall white tower has three faces: golden, white and black.
He often said, "Be free form traditional rules." and "Express yourself freely."
